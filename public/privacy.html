Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect: (a) Account data you provide (email, name, password hash); (b) Usage data (API calls, feature usage); (c) Device data (platform, OS version) for crash reporting. We do NOT sell your data.

2. How We Use Your Data

To provide and improve the service, send transactional emails (account alerts, receipts), comply with legal obligations, and detect security threats. We never use your data for advertising profiling without consent.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with: Stripe (payment processing), Sentry (error monitoring — PII stripped before transmission), and as required by law. No data is sold to third parties.

4. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at GET /api/v1/gdpr/data-export
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data at DELETE /api/v1/gdpr/data-delete
  • Portability: Export in JSON format
  • Opt-out: Manage consent at /privacy-settings

5. Data Retention

Active accounts: retained while account is active. Deleted accounts: purged within 30 days. Financial records: 7 years (legal requirement).

6. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Data in transit uses TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted. We conduct regular security audits and penetration tests.

7. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies by default. Analytics/marketing cookies require your consent. See our Cookie Policy.

8. Contact

Data Controller: Sianlk. Privacy enquiries: privacy@sianlk.com. Response within 30 days guaranteed.
